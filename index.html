My Story

In the short time I have lived, I have had a myriad of experiences that have shaped and molded me. Some in a positive way and some in a painful way.

My school years were a difficult journey, mentally and emotionally I felt very insecure.

I have since learned so much in how to cope with loss and pain at a deep level and how to restore the years the locusts have eaten.

I have been drawn to sit with people, hear their stories and partner with them to develop a plan to restore areas of being uncertain and unsure where to go next.

I went to get a degree and to be certified along with countless therapy sessions and being open to learn more by reading a lot of books.

I have had mentors, professors, and small group leaders speak into my life and restore so much of my past to become the man I was created with the gifting my creator endowed me with.

I have a huge heart for men who maybe didn't have a father they admired. All men have a father wound. I had no idea how to be a dad and although I made many mistakes as a dad I also learned from a lot of men I looked up to who coached me to become a better man in each stage of my manhood.
